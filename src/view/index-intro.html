The purpose of this site is to build a reliable and verifiable database of famous persons, with their birth time.

It currently contains the data used to perform statistical tests on astrology, between 1955 and 1996 ("historical data").
These data were gathered by Michel and Françoise Gauquelin, Arno Müller, Suitbert Ertel and 3 groups of skeptics: Comité Para - Belgium 1976, CSICOP - U.S. 1979, CFEPP - France 1996.
