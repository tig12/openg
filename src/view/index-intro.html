The purpose of this site is to build a database of famous persons, with their birth place, date and time.
Emphasis is put on reliability and verifiability of the data.

It currently contains data used to perform statistical tests on astrology,
gathered by Michel and Françoise Gauquelin, Arno Müller, Suitbert Ertel
and three groups of skeptics - between 1955 and 1996.
This gives {{numberFormat .N_time}} persons with birth time and {{numberFormat .N_day}} without birth time.
