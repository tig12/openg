Files BC.yml - Open Gauquelin Database

The file called BC.yml permits to document precisely the link between a person of the database and his / her birth certificate.
Here is an example: 
#
# Transcription of a birth certificate
# This document is released under the terms of licence
# Creative Commons CC BY-SA 4.0 (Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0) 
# https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/
#

header:
  history:
    -
      actor: Thierry Graff
      date: '2014-11-19' # 00:19:23+02:00
      action: Locate and transcribe the act
      
opengauquelin:
  projects: 
    - french-math
   
source:
  civil-registry:
    name: 4E216-33 Naissance État civil 02/01/1871 - 08/04/1871
    place:
      name: Saint-Affrique
      cy: FR
      c2: '12'
    web: 
      url: http://archives.aveyron.fr/ark:/11971/vta52df831877e0c/daogrp/0#id:1722182262
      page: 6 / 40
  
  document-creation:
    date: '1871-01-09 15:00'
    place: Saint-Affrique
  notes: Acte N° 11, feuillet N° 4
  
transcription:
  name:
    official:         
      family: Borel
      given: Félix Edouard Justin Emile
  
  sex: M
  
  birth:
    date: '1871-01-09 04:00'
    place:
      name: Saint-Affrique
      cy: FR
      c2: '12'
      
extras:
  name:
    given: Emile
    family: Borel
  occus:
    - mathematician
